The locked rules

Volt: badges, tech, box interior only. Max ~10% of any surface. Never a background.
Archivo only. Headlines: Expanded Bold caps. Subheads: Semibold. Body: Regular. No serifs, ever.
1. Short sentences. Declarative. 2. Proof, never adjectives. 3. Talk to him, not at him. 4. Confident, never loud. No exclamation marks.
Wear Your Change — motto. Sign-offs, box, footer, story end. Built right, not loud — product philosophy. About, box exterior, headlines. The 7th — drop day + newsletter name. A gift. From you, to your feet. — ribbon tag only.
Appears when something happens: 404, empty cart, order placed, drop day, coming soon. Never on: box exterior, hang tag front, logo lockup, product ads, homepage hero. Emotion = eyes + squash/stretch only. One appearance per page/post.
No fake MRPs. No permanent sales. No fabricated reviews. "Wrinkle-resistant", never "wrinkle-free" (until the 30-day test is filmed). Competitors by price tier, never by name. Factory story = About + paid ads, never the homepage opener.
